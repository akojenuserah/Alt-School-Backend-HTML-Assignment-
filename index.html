Liver and Potato Purée for Babies (6+ Months)


A soft, nutritious, and iron-rich meal made with tender liver and creamy potatoes. This purée is perfect for babies over 6 months who are starting solids and need healthy nutrients for growth and development.

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

20 mins

Servings

2 Portions

Difficulty

Easy


Ingredients


  • 1 small potato, peeled and chopped
  • 50g chicken or beef liver
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • ½ cup clean water
  • Breast milk or formula (optional for thinning)
  • A pinch of mild herbs (optional)
  • Breast milk, formula, or extra water (to thin if needed)

Tips To Note


  1. Liver and potato purée is a great source of iron and protein
  2. Ensure liver is fully cooked
  3. Serve warm, not hot
  4. Store for only 24 hours
